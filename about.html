About NexArt

Building the future of female artists in Web3

Our Mission

NexArt is building a platform to empower female artists in the Web3 ecosystem. We believe blockchain technology and NFTs offer unprecedented opportunities for artists to own, showcase, and monetize their creative work.

Our mission is to create an inclusive space where female artists can thrive, connect with global audiences, and build sustainable creative careers through NFT technology.

NexArt is actively building a curated platform with select pioneer artists, creating an empowering ecosystem for female creators in Web3.

Our Mission

To empower female artists by providing them with the tools, community, and platform to thrive in Web3. We're committed to breaking down barriers and creating opportunities for women in the digital art space.

Our Vision

A world where female artists have equal access to opportunities in the digital art market, where their work is valued and celebrated, and where Web3 technology serves as a bridge to global audiences.

Meet the Founder

Tia founded NexArt to create a more inclusive space for female artists in Web3. A UK-based entrepreneur with master's degrees in Education (UCL) and Film Studies (UEA), she brings a unique perspective that bridges art, technology, and community.

Through NexArt, she is building infrastructure to empower artists with the tools and opportunities they need to thrive in the evolving digital art landscape.

Why NFTs for Female Artists?

Ownership & Control

Artists retain full control and provenance of their work. Every piece is authenticated on the blockchain, ensuring true ownership and creative rights.

Direct Connection

Connect with collectors without intermediaries. Build direct relationships with your audience and community without traditional gatekeepers.

Fair Compensation

Transparent transactions and ongoing royalties. Artists earn fairly from initial sales and continue to benefit from secondary market transactions.
