## Email Sending Tips

### Best Times to Send:
- **Twitter/Instagram:** 12pm-3pm, 7pm-9pm (local time)
- **Email:** Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-11am
- **LinkedIn:** Tuesday-Wednesday, 9am-12pm

### Follow-up Schedule:
1. **Initial contact:** Day 1
2. **First follow-up:** Day 5-7 (if no response)
3. **Second follow-up:** Day 14 (if still no response)
4. **Final follow-up:** Day 21-30

### Personalization Checklist:
- [ ] Artist's name
- [ ] Specific artwork mentioned
- [ ] Why their style fits NexArt
- [ ] Relevant to their current projects/interests
- [ ] Platform-appropriate tone (casual for IG, professional for LinkedIn)

### Red Flags to Avoid:
- ‚ùå Generic "Dear Artist" messages
- ‚ùå Copy-paste obvious templates
- ‚ùå Pushy or aggressive tone
- ‚ùå Promises of guaranteed sales
- ‚ùå Asking for money upfront

### Success Metrics:
- **Target:** 50 artists contacted
- **Expected response rate:** 10-20% (5-10 responses)
- **Goal conversions:** 3-5 signed artists
- **Timeframe:** 4-6 weeks
